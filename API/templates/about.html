What is a DrugFinder?

DrugFinder is an AI-powered web application, that allows the users to find the best drug for their disease. It makes use of one of the most powerful NLP algorithm to detect the disease in the text.

How does it work?

The process of finding a drug consists of three steps

Enter your text

The user enters the doctor's note through the web application

Let the AI find your disease

The AI algorithm detects your disease from the text.

See the best drug for you!

The result of the AI algorithm is matched with the database of drugs and the result is ready for you!
